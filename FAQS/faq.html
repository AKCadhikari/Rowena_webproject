Rowena Perfume Store | FAQs

What ingredients are used in your perfumes?
Our perfumes are made from high-quality, natural ingredients including essential oils, alcohol, and water. We use a blend of botanical and synthetic elements to create unique, long-lasting scents.
How long does the fragrance last?
The longevity of our perfumes varies depending on the specific fragrance and your skin type. Generally, our perfumes last between 6 to 8 hours.
Are your perfumes cruelty-free?
Yes, all of our perfumes are 100% cruelty-free. We do not test on animals at any stage of our production process.
Do you offer sample sizes?
Yes, we offer sample sizes of all our fragrances so you can try them out before purchasing a full-size bottle.
